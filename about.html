About Black Backpack

Crafting premium custom backpacks for businesses worldwide since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Black Backpack emerged from a simple vision: to create the world's finest custom backpacks for businesses seeking exceptional quality and innovative design. What started as a small manufacturing workshop has evolved into a leading B2B backpack manufacturer serving clients across six continents.

Our journey began when our founder recognized a gap in the market for truly customizable, high-quality backpacks that could meet the diverse needs of modern businesses. From corporate gifts to specialized industry equipment, we saw the potential to transform how companies approach their backpack requirements.

Today, we proudly manufacture over 200,000+ custom backpacks monthly, each one crafted with the same attention to detail and commitment to excellence that defined our early days. Our success is built on three fundamental pillars: uncompromising quality, innovative design, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To empower businesses worldwide with custom backpack solutions that perfectly balance functionality, durability, and style. We strive to exceed expectations through innovative design, superior craftsmanship, and personalized service that transforms ideas into reality.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in custom backpack manufacturing, recognized for our commitment to sustainability, innovation, and customer success. We envision a future where every business can access premium, personalized backpack solutions that enhance their brand and serve their unique needs.
